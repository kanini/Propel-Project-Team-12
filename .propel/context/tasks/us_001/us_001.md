# User Story - US_001

## Story ID

- ID: US_001

## Story Title

- Patient Account Registration with Email Validation

## Description

- As a new patient, I want to create an account with my email address and a secure password, so that I can access the platform to book appointments and manage my healthcare information.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am on the landing page, **When** I click "Sign Up" and enter a valid email address and password meeting security requirements (minimum 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special character), **Then** the system creates my account and sends a verification email within 2 minutes.

2. **Given** I have received the verification email, **When** I click the verification link within 24 hours, **Then** my account is activated and I am redirected to the sign-in page with a success message.

3. **Given** I am registering a new account, **When** I enter an email address that is already registered, **Then** the system displays an error message "This email is already registered" and offers a link to sign in or reset password.

4. **Given** I am entering a password during registration, **When** my password does not meet the security requirements, **Then** the system displays inline validation errors specifying which requirements are not met (e.g., "Password must contain at least 1 uppercase letter").

5. **Given** I submit the registration form, **When** any required field is empty or invalid, **Then** the system prevents submission and highlights all fields with validation errors without losing entered data.

6. **Given** I click the verification link, **When** more than 24 hours have passed since registration, **Then** the system displays "Link expired" and offers to resend a new verification email.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a user attempts to register with a malformed email address? (System rejects with inline validation: "Please enter a valid email address")
- How does the system handle registration attempts from temporary/disposable email domains? (System accepts but may flag for review in future phases)
- What happens if the verification email fails to send? (System displays error with retry option and logs failure for support)
- How does the system handle concurrent registration attempts with the same email? (First successful submission wins; second receives "email already registered")
