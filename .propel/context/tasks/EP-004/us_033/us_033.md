# User Story - US_033

## Story ID

* ID: US_033

## Story Title

* AI Conversational Intake

## Description

* As a patient, I want to complete my pre-visit intake through an AI conversational interface that guides me through health history, medications, allergies, and visit concerns, so that I can provide my health information naturally and efficiently.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have an upcoming appointment, **When** I select "AI Intake" on the intake page, **Then** the AI conversational interface loads with a welcome message and begins guiding me through structured health data collection.

2. **Given** the AI is asking questions, **When** I respond in natural language (e.g., "I take metformin 500mg twice daily"), **Then** the AI extracts structured data (medication: metformin, dose: 500mg, frequency: twice daily) and confirms understanding.

3. **Given** the conversation is progressing, **When** the AI has collected all required categories (symptoms, history, medications, allergies, concerns), **Then** a summary is displayed for my review before submission.

4. **Given** AI confidence drops below 70%, **When** the system detects low extraction confidence (AIR-S03), **Then** the AI suggests switching to manual form mode while preserving all entered data.

5. **Given** token budget enforcement, **When** any single AI request is made, **Then** the request does not exceed 4000 tokens (AIR-O01) to manage costs and latency.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when the AI cannot understand a patient response? AI requests clarification with a rephrased question; after 3 consecutive failures, offers manual form option.
* How does the system handle patients who provide minimal responses? AI uses follow-up prompts to elicit more detail while remaining patient-friendly.
