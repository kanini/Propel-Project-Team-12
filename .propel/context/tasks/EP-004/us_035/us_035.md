# User Story - US_035

## Story ID

* ID: US_035

## Story Title

* Intake Mode Switching and Response Editing

## Description

* As a patient, I want to freely switch between AI conversational and manual form intake while preserving all entered data, and to edit my responses without help, so that I have full control over my intake experience.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am in AI conversational intake, **When** I click "Switch to Manual Form", **Then** the manual form loads pre-populated with all data extracted from the AI conversation with no data loss.

2. **Given** I am in manual form intake, **When** I click "Switch to AI Intake", **Then** the AI conversation resumes with awareness of already-entered form data, avoiding re-asking completed sections.

3. **Given** I have submitted my intake, **When** I access my intake responses before the appointment, **Then** I can edit any field without requiring staff assistance and the changes are saved immediately.

4. **Given** mode switching is in progress, **When** the data transfer occurs, **Then** a brief loading indicator appears and all data mappings are preserved accurately between structured form fields and AI extraction.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when AI-extracted data cannot cleanly map to form fields? Unmapped data is placed in a "Additional Notes" section for manual review.
* How does the system handle switching modes during a partially completed intake? All partial data from the current mode is preserved and transferred to the new mode.
