# User Story - US_025

## Story ID

* ID: US_025

## Story Title

* Waitlist Enrollment

## Description

* As a patient, I want to join a waitlist when my preferred appointment slot is unavailable, so that I can be notified when a matching slot opens up.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** my preferred time slot is unavailable, **When** I click "Join Waitlist", **Then** an enrollment form displays asking for preferred date/time range and notification preferences (SMS/Email/Both).

2. **Given** I complete the waitlist form, **When** I submit, **Then** a waitlist entry is created with priority timestamp, and I receive a confirmation notification via my selected channel.

3. **Given** I am already on the waitlist for the same provider and date, **When** I attempt to join again, **Then** the system informs me of my existing waitlist position and offers to update preferences.

4. **Given** I am on the waitlist, **When** I view My Appointments, **Then** my waitlist entries are shown in a "Waitlist" tab with position, provider, preferred range, and status.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when 50+ patients are on the waitlist for the same slot? Entries are ordered by priority timestamp (FIFO), and only the first eligible patient is notified when a slot opens.
* How does the system handle waitlist for a provider who becomes unavailable? Waitlist entries are marked as "Provider Unavailable" and patients are notified to re-enroll.
