# User Story - US_028

## Story ID

* ID: US_028

## Story Title

* Appointment Confirmation PDF and Email

## Description

* As a patient, I want to receive an appointment confirmation PDF via email after booking, so that I have a printable record of my scheduled visit.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have successfully booked an appointment, **When** the booking is confirmed, **Then** the system generates a PDF using QuestPDF containing appointment date/time, provider name, specialty, location, visit reason, and a unique confirmation number.

2. **Given** the PDF is generated, **When** the confirmation email is sent, **Then** the PDF is attached to the email along with a summary of the appointment details in the email body.

3. **Given** the email is sent, **When** I check my inbox, **Then** I receive the confirmation within 2 minutes of booking with a professional email template including the platform branding.

4. **Given** I need the confirmation again, **When** I access My Appointments and select the appointment, **Then** I can download the confirmation PDF directly from the appointment details view.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when PDF generation fails? Appointment is still confirmed; a retry job re-generates the PDF within 5 minutes and sends it.
* How does the system handle email delivery failure? The notification service retries with exponential backoff (max 3 retries) and the PDF remains downloadable from the platform.
