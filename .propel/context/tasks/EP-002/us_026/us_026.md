# User Story - US_026

## Story ID

* ID: US_026

## Story Title

* Dynamic Preferred Slot Swap

## Description

* As a patient, I want to book an available slot while selecting a preferred unavailable slot, so that the system automatically swaps me to the preferred time when it becomes available.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am booking an appointment, **When** I select an available slot, **Then** I can optionally mark a preferred unavailable slot as my swap preference.

2. **Given** I have a swap preference set, **When** the preferred slot becomes available (via cancellation or schedule change), **Then** the system automatically books me into the preferred slot, releases my original slot, and sends a swap confirmation notification.

3. **Given** the preferred slot becomes available, **When** another patient books it first (race condition), **Then** the system maintains my original booking and notifies me that the swap was not possible.

4. **Given** I decide to cancel my swap preference, **When** I update my appointment preferences, **Then** the swap preference is removed and my original booking remains unchanged.

5. **Given** a swap executes successfully, **When** I check My Appointments, **Then** the appointment reflects the new time, my calendar integration is updated, and the original slot appears as available to other patients.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when both the original and preferred slots are for the same day? Swap proceeds normally with both slot transitions handled atomically.
* How does the system handle swap when the patient has cancelled their original appointment? Swap is skipped since no active appointment exists.
