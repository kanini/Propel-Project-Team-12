# User Story - US_038

## Story ID

* ID: US_038

## Story Title

* No-Show Risk Assessment Engine

## Description

* As a Staff member, I want a rule-based no-show risk score calculated for each appointment, so that high-risk patients can receive targeted engagement to reduce no-shows.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** an appointment is booked, **When** the booking is confirmed, **Then** a no-show risk score (0-100) is calculated within 100ms (NFR-016) using weighted factors: appointment lead time, previous no-show history, and confirmation response rate.

2. **Given** risk factors are configurable (TR-020), **When** I inspect the scoring engine, **Then** each factor has a configurable weight and the calculation is deterministic (rule-based, not AI).

3. **Given** a high-risk score (e.g., >70), **When** Staff views the appointment, **Then** a visual risk indicator (red/amber/green badge) is displayed alongside the appointment details.

4. **Given** patient history is updated, **When** a no-show is recorded or a confirmation is received, **Then** the NoShowHistory record is updated and future risk scores reflect the new data.

## Edge Cases

* What happens for first-time patients with no history? Default risk score is calculated using only appointment lead time; no-show history weight evaluates to neutral.
* How does the system handle risk score for same-day walk-ins? Walk-in appointments receive a fixed low-risk score since they are already on-site.
