# User Story - US_037

## Story ID

* ID: US_037

## Story Title

* Multi-Channel Automated Reminder System

## Description

* As a patient, I want to receive automated SMS and email reminders at configurable intervals before my appointment, so that I am reminded of upcoming visits and can reduce no-show risk.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have a booked appointment, **When** a configured reminder interval is reached (e.g., 48h, 24h, 2h before), **Then** the system sends a reminder via both SMS (Twilio) and Email (SendGrid) with appointment date, time, provider, and location.

2. **Given** a reminder is triggered, **When** it is delivered, **Then** the delivery occurs within 30 seconds of the scheduled trigger time (NFR-017).

3. **Given** a delivery fails, **When** the SMS or email service returns an error, **Then** the system retries with exponential backoff (max 3 retries: 1min, 4min, 16min) and logs the failure.

4. **Given** reminder intervals are configurable, **When** an Admin changes reminder timing in system settings, **Then** future appointments use the new intervals while already-scheduled reminders remain unchanged.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when a patient has no phone number? Only email reminders are sent; the SMS notification is skipped with a log entry.
* How does the system handle appointment cancellation after reminders are scheduled? Pending reminders are cancelled and no further notifications are sent.
