# User Story - US_041

## Story ID

* ID: US_041

## Story Title

* Waitlist Slot Availability Notifications

## Description

* As a patient on a waitlist, I want to be notified when my preferred slot becomes available and confirm or decline the swap, so that I can take advantage of newly opened appointment times.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am on a waitlist and my preferred slot becomes available, **When** a cancellation opens the matching slot, **Then** the system sends a notification via my preferred channel (SMS/Email) with slot details and confirm/decline options.

2. **Given** I receive the notification, **When** I click "Confirm", **Then** the system books me into the available slot, removes me from the waitlist, and sends a booking confirmation.

3. **Given** I receive the notification, **When** I click "Decline", **Then** the system keeps me on the waitlist and offers the slot to the next eligible patient.

4. **Given** I do not respond within a configured timeout (e.g., 30 minutes), **When** the timeout expires, **Then** the system treats it as a decline and offers the slot to the next patient on the waitlist.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when multiple patients are on the waitlist for the same slot? The first patient (by priority timestamp) is notified; if they decline or timeout, the next patient is notified sequentially.
* How does the system handle a slot that opens and closes (re-booked) before the notification is delivered? Notification includes real-time availability check on confirm — if slot is gone, patient is notified and kept on waitlist.
