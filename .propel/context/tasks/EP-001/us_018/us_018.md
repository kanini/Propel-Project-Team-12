# User Story - US_018

## Story ID

* ID: US_018

## Story Title

* Patient Account Registration

## Description

* As a new patient, I want to register an account with email validation, name, date of birth, and contact information, so that I can access the platform securely.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am on the registration page, **When** I enter valid personal information (name, date of birth, email, phone, password), **Then** the system creates my account with status "pending" and sends a verification email within 2 minutes.

2. **Given** I receive the verification email, **When** I click the verification link, **Then** my account is activated and I am redirected to the login page with a success message.

3. **Given** I am registering, **When** I enter an email that is already registered, **Then** the system displays an error message and offers a password recovery option without revealing whether the email exists (security).

4. **Given** I am entering a password, **When** the password does not meet security requirements (8+ characters, 1 uppercase, 1 number, 1 special character), **Then** inline validation displays specific missing requirements below the password field.

5. **Given** the verification link was sent, **When** I click it after 24 hours, **Then** the system displays "Link expired" and offers to resend a new verification email.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when a user requests multiple verification emails within 5 minutes? Rate limiting allows max 3 requests per 5 minutes to prevent abuse.
* How does the system handle registration with invalid date of birth (future date)? Inline validation rejects future dates with clear error message.
