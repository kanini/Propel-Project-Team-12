# User Story - US_031

## Story ID

* ID: US_031

## Story Title

* Patient Arrival Status Marking

## Description

* As a Staff member, I want to mark patients as "Arrived" for their appointments, so that the queue and appointment status accurately reflect patient presence.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am on the Arrival Management page, **When** I search for a patient with a today's appointment, **Then** the patient's appointment details are displayed with a "Mark Arrived" button.

2. **Given** I click "Mark Arrived", **When** the action is confirmed, **Then** the appointment status updates to "Arrived", the patient is added to the queue, and an audit log entry is created.

3. **Given** a patient has no appointment today, **When** I search for them on the arrival page, **Then** the system indicates no appointment found and offers to create a walk-in booking.

4. **Given** RBAC is enforced, **When** a Patient user attempts to self-check-in, **Then** the arrival marking functionality is not available — only Staff can mark arrivals.

## Edge Cases

* What happens when Staff marks a patient arrived who was already marked? System displays "Patient already marked as arrived" and prevents duplicate status change.
* How does the system handle marking arrival for a cancelled appointment? System prevents arrival marking for cancelled appointments with "Appointment was cancelled" message.
